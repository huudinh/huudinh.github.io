FAQ – Câu hỏi thường gặp

  • What is a brand mention for SEO in 2025?

    A brand mention is when your business or service is referenced online with or without a link. It can appear in articles, blogs, forums, social media, videos, podcasts, or other platforms. In 2025, both Google and AI platforms use these mentions as trust and authority signals to rank sites higher.

  • How do brand mentions impact SEO?

    Brand mentions can boost online authority, improve visibility, and drive referral traffic. Search engines and the AI ecosystem factor in the frequency, context, and sentiment of mentions when ranking sites.

  • What are the different types of brand mentions?

    Common types include social media mentions, PR mentions, blog and forum mentions, reviews and testimonials, podcast and video mentions, and broadcast coverage.

  • What are some strategies to gain more brand mentions?

    Create valuable content, collaborate with influencers, publish guest posts, run PR campaigns, encourage user-generated content, be active in online communities, deliver excellent customer service, and participate in industry events.
