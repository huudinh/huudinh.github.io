Grid Classes

Grid system has four classes:

  1. .col- (extra small devices - screen width less than 576px)
  2. .col-md- (medium devices - screen width equal to or greater than 812px)
  3. .col-lg- (large devices - screen width equal to or greater than 992px)
  4. .col-xl- (xlarge devices - screen width equal to or greater than 1200px)
